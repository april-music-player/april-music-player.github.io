Welcome to April Music Player

April Music Player is more than a music app — it’s a learning companion! Dive into features like interactive lyrics, personalized vocabulary tracking, and music downloads that make language learning fun and seamless.

Why Choose April Music Player?

  • 🎵 Sync and interact with LRC lyrics
  • 📝 Take notes on lyrics and build your personal dictionary
  • 🔄 Download music and lyrics with integrated tools
  • 🌐 Multilingual support for language learners
  • 💻 Built using PyQt6 with a modern, user-friendly design
